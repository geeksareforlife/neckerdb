The blue and gold are the colours of the St Alban’s cross (for our city’s patron saint) and the green for Charles “Jim” Dymoke Green, our third leader and editor of ‘The Scouter’. Our hut is named after him, but he’s more famously remembered by the Jim Green Gate at Gilwell Park.
