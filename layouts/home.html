What is NeckerDB?

From bold stripes to checkerboard designs, every Scout or Guide group has a unique necker - and a story behind it.

The Necker Archive is a community-led project to document and showcase the rich diversity of neckerchiefs used by:

  • Scout and Guide Groups
  • Districts, Counties, and Regions
  • Events, camps, and international exchanges

Whether you're reminiscing, researching, or representing your group, you're in the right place.
